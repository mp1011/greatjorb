Job Description

You will work as a key member of an Agile engineering team and be involved in all aspects of designing, developing, and supporting world-class REST APIs and web-based software products. You will be working collaboratively with Product Management, Quality Assurance, and Support/Services teams.

We offer a competitive salary, generous medical, dental, vision, and life insurance, and stock options. This position can be remote or you can work out of our Las Vegas Office.

Required Skills:

  • C#
  • .NET Core
  • REST API Development
  • ASP.NET Core
  • TSQL/SQL
  • GitHub
  • Visual Studio
  • SSMS
  • API Testing tools such as Postman and Insomnia

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Design and Develop high-performance back-end systems and APIs used by hundreds of thousands of users globally.
  • Use state-of-the-art back-end technologies to develop highly available applications and APIs that can scale in Cascade's multi-datacenter production environment.
  • Write effective unit tests and integration tests to ensure high-quality software delivery, sprint after sprint. Leverage automation testing methods to increase test coverage and build confidence in Cascade's continuous delivery pipeline.
  • Be an effective contributor to Cascade's Agile teams. Participate vigorously in SCRUM ceremonies and help the team to get to "done-done" consistently.
  • Collaborate on effective development practices and evangelize within and across teams.
  • Continually evaluate emerging frameworks and technologies to identify opportunities, trends, and best practices that strengthen Cascade's development teams.

Nice to Have Skills:

  • Experience with AWS Lambda
  • Experience with server-side development in other object-oriented languages such as Java
  • Experience with SDLC methodologies such as Agile or SCRUM
  • Experience with Continuous Integration
  • Experience with software test automation
  • Powershell
  • Bash

The ideal candidate will possess the following performance and personal characteristics:

  • A passion for demonstrating the art of the possible in terms of how data and technology enable world-class customer experiences and drive customer loyalty.
  • Track record of success influencing without direct authority and balancing between competing priorities while remaining flexible and creative.
  • Strong ability to build and strengthen team engagement and align resources to strategy, key tactics, and measures; proven ability to establish clear aligned goals and objectives with a timely feedback loop.
