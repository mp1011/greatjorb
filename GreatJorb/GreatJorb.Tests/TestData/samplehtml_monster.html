Job Description

The Architect role is to develop and maintain the high-level target state design for the technology architecture under the guidance and direction of the 1ST management. The Architect will provide technical leadership and consulting across tile organization, on strategic decisions and architectural methodologies. The Architect will gain organizational commitment to the strategic technology roadmap and initiate and participate in transformation Initiatives to evaluate technologies and methods for Implementing these plans. The key tasks and responsibilities include:

  • Demonstrates understanding of corporate goals and objectives and clients' business needs by using IT methodologies to ensure appropriate solutions are implemented.
  • Develops high-level architecture design documents to facilitate integration of in-house development project and/or vendor-based application within the existing enterprise architecture.
  • Develops/Maintains "Best Practices" policies/procedures for the technical implementations developed within the IST team.
  • Develops detailed data models for logical and conceptual database designs. Applies data modelling techniques and enterprise data standards to highly complex business requirements.
  • Performs initial design, problem solving and reviews of software architecture that conforms with
  • the strategic direction of corporate ICT strategy.
  • Performs analysis oversight during detailed design of systems that conform with the strategic direction of IST office.
  • Provides solutions to highly complex projects to meet corporate goals that incluc1e that are primarily strategic and long-term in scope having corporate-wide impact.
  • Ensures financial targets are met by meeting budget goals and objectives.
  • Coaches, mentors, delegates, and provides technical leadership lo developers to facilitate their development. Fosters high performing technical teams.
  • Coordinates cross-functional facilitation to resolve issues.
  • Obtain more effective overall operations by recommending best practice technical solutions.
  • Working with IST management team, ensures application development and task durations are appropriately assigned.
  • Proposes changes to improve work and process flows and be a change catalyst. constantly considers new ways of operating and actively leads appropriate sustainable change initiatives consistent with the ICT strategy. Adapts priorities and actions lo changing business situations.
  • Ensure that the security, Information, business, Integration, and application architecture interests are considered.

Minimum Qualifications

  • 10+ years of hands-on design and implementation of business solutions.
  • Hands-on experience in Microsoft .NET technology stack with demonstrated progressive levels of technical leadership, responsibility, knowledge, and experience.
  • Prior experience as a technical architect, senior software engineer, or senior systems designer
  • In depth experience using the following: WCF, C#.NET, Distributed Systems,
  • SOA, Web Services, ASP NET, SQL Server 2008
  • Experience in Cloud computing, preferably Microsoft Office365 and Azure
  • Experience in CMS platforms, preferably Sitecore CMS
  • Excellent interpersonal, listening, written and oral communication skills.
  • Demonstrated decision-making ability.
  • Excellent analytical ability
  • PRINCE2 and rm certifications preferred.
  • Agile/ SCRUM certification strongly preferred
